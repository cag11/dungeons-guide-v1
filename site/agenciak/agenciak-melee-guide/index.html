    Armor

    Best armor options for melee-focused builds

    Wither Armor

    • Lifesteal aura (heals you for 6% of your weapon's direct damage (so enchants like Shockwave, Swirling or Thundering don't fuel it, if you're using lifesteal armor, these are the enchants you want to avoid, also it's an aura, meaning that your allies also benefit from it)
    • +35% Damage reduction

    Spider Armor

    • Lifesteal aura
    • +25% faster attack speed

    Renegade Armor

    • +20% weapon damage boost aura
    • +25% faster attack speed
    • +35% damage reduction

    Enchantments

    Cooldown - mandatory, allows for infinite deathcap mushroom and iron hide amulet at high power levels

    Potion barrier - very good, gives you 90% DR for 9 seconds every time your health potion is used, very strong when paired with Refreshment

    Deflecting - very good, has a 45% to block 100% damage from incoming projectiles and deflect them to their source

    Chilling - very good, slows enemies (Triggers every 2 seconds)

    Snowball - very good, stuns one enemy for 1 second every second (it can save you in close meetings with creepers)

    Death barter - good, allows you to respawn with 100% health, the downside of it is that discards everything you earned during the mission, it's not the best possible option for builds focusing on not dying at all (melee/tank builds)

    Cowardice - good, if you have full HP, you deal 40% more damage, melee builds take a lot of damage but with solid healing it's a good DPS booster

    Surprise gift - only in specific builds, you have 50% chance to create a random consumable item, but that also could be used by itself, but combined with Refreshment it can be really helpful, often used alongside Potion Barrier in health potion spam builds

    Reckless - only in specific builds, you have 40% less HP, but deal 90% more damage, it should be used only in builds with a lot of damage reduction

    Ranged Weapons

    Your ranged weapon doesn't really matter in melee builds, you could preferably use a one-shot bow (elite power bow with enchants like + + ) to easily clear out stronger enemies or an imploding crossbow with Refreshment to utilise potion enchants like barrier more effectively
