    Beginner's Guide - Rolling builds

    What is a "rolling build"?

    The core of it being rolling build is Burstbowstring paired with Cooldown shot and a Light feather, how does it work? Burstbowstring triggers every time you roll, shooting out 1 fully charged arrow per enemy (up to 3 arrows), each of them triggers Cooldownshot speeding up your artifact cooldown 1.5s per charged shot, Light feather has a 3s cooldown, which means that you get to roll constantly shooting 3 charged arrows (which shortens your cooldown by 4.5s, allowing for a second roll using the feather right after) every time you do it with the only condition being close to at least 2 enemies

    Remember that you need a ton of arrows for it to work, that's why you need Looting or Infinity

    Ranged Weapon

    Best options for a rolling bow

    Winter's Touch

    If your build uses any quiver artifacts, this is what you're looking for

    • Shoots out 3 arrows when charged (In a rolling build it means 9 arrows for every roll, because 3 arrows per 1 shot and Burstbowstring provides 3 shots)
    • Slows down hit enemies

    Pink Scoundrel

    • ricochets after hitting an enemy
    • has a chance to "enrage" mobs (makes them attack everything instead of focusing on the player, other enemies, passive animals but still can target players)

    Bubble Burster

    • ricochets after hitting an enemy
    • traps enemies in bubbles

    Enchantments

    Burstbowstring - mandatory

    Cooldown shot - mandatory

    Infinity - if you use quivers, has a chance to not use up your arrow

    Looting - if you don't use quivers,

    Multishot - if you use Winter's touch, has a 40% to turn each arrow you shoot out into 5 projectiles, it's very effective on Winter's touch, because it shoots out 3 arrows (meaning each of them has a 40% chance for it activate)

    Tempo theft - good, slows down enemies and speeds you up

    Wild rage - good, enrages mobs

    Voidstrike shot - good, applies a damage multiplier, weaker than melee Voidstrike (added)

    Melee weapon

    You can exploit the fact that you constantly roll by using weapons with "jungle poison" transfer their damage into the poison, paired with which makes the poison deal up to 600% damage you are able to oneshot most bosses easily

    Encrusted Anchor

    Very high damage and area

    Armor

    Best armor choices for a rolling build

    Shadow Walker

    For normal rolling

    • invulnerability while rolling
    • +35% damage reduction
    • -50% rolling cooldown

    + + / / + ( Lightning focus only with Thundering Quiver or Satchel of Elements)

    Glow Squid Armor

    For normal rolling

    • invulnerability while rolling
    • +15% movespeed aura
    • shoots out ink, making enemies miss some hits

    + + / / + ( Lightning focus only with Thundering Quiver or Satchel of Elements)

    Splendid Robe

    For artifact spam

    • +40% artifact cooldown
    • +30% melee damage
    • +50% artifact damage
