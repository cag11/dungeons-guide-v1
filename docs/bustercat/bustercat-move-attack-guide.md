## üéØ What is Move-Attacking?

Move-attacking is an advanced technique that allows you to move around while using the anchor weapon. This technique is essential for maintaining mobility during combat while still dealing damage.

---

## üéÆ How to Move-Attack with the Anchor

### For the Host Player

**Step-by-step process:**

1. **Hold** the melee attack button
2. **Click** light feather milliseconds after (ideally)
3. **Let go** of the attack button mid-air or before you touch the ground
4. You can now **move around** or **dodge projectiles** while waiting for the anchor to land

**Requirements:**
- At least **2 mobs nearby** 
- **Burst Bowstring** enchantment
- **Light Feather** artifact

---

### For Non-Host Players

If you aren't the host, the timing is different:

1. **Click** the melee button
2. **Use feather** right after
3. **No need** for Burst Bowstring or even a feather for basic movement

---

## üí° Tips & Tricks

!!! tip "Timing is Everything"
    The key to successful move-attacking is the timing between clicking the melee button and activating the light feather. Practice the timing in lower difficulties first!

!!! info "Host vs Non-Host"
    The technique works differently depending on whether you're hosting the game or joining someone else's game. Make sure to practice both methods!

!!! warning "Mob Requirements"
    For hosts, you need at least 2 mobs nearby with burst bowstring to execute this technique properly. Plan your positioning accordingly!
