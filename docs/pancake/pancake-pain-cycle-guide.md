## Pain Cycle damage boost on weapons.

Assuming it stays lined up to trigger on the last hit of the combo.

Pain Cycle is additive so it scales badly with things like sharpness or strength adding the percentages together instead of multiplying each other.

Katana: 84.7%
Mace: 103.7%
Spear: 90.9% (Whispering Spear will off-set it due to the echo)
Starless Night: 87.3%
